Performance Requirements

1. The car must not move outside the track more than 3 times per lap.
   Definintion: When all four tires are outside the marked edge, the car is considered off track.

2. The car must identify different road signs and react accordingly.

3. The car must evade the obsticle provided in the track.
   Definition: If the car touches the obstacle, then it fails this requirement.

4. All power must be supplied from 2 seperate litium-ion batteries.

5. The total power consumption of the design cannot exceed the maximum capacity of the battery. 

6. The design must be transferable to any medium sized RC car without modifying the servo or motor.
